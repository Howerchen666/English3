Steve Jobs biography by Isaacson, Walter

This biography was written by Isaacson Walter and it is based on more than 40 interviews with Steve Jobs. In this book, the author introducted Steve job's entire life. How he started Apple and all of the major events that happend. By reading this book you will get a pretty good idea of Apple's entire history.




Designed by Apple in California

This book documents 20 years of Apple's design through 450 images of both old and new products. The book "Designed by Apple in California," which covers devices from the iMac in 1998 through the Apple Pencil in 2015, also details the resources and methods that Apple's design team employed throughout its two-decade history of invention. This book was written to honor Steve Jobs.

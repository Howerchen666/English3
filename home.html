Welcome to this site!

Apple recently wrapped off its autumn special event and released a number of items to the general public and its loyal customer base. These days, the new Apple product is all over the internet. The public has come to associate Apple with high-end brands, and the corporation is now well-known for its line of computers. It piqued my interest, and I started to wonder how Apple's brand image had evolved over the previous 40 years. When Apple debuted its first product 40 years ago, how did people perceive the company? How Apple's brand image has changed over the past 40 years is the main topic of this website.
